About Us

Yalla Kampi is an online space where you can find everything you need about camping. On this platform, you can discover new and secure places where you can camp. You can also buy some of your favorite items such as clothing and camping material.

What Do we offer ?

  • Our website provides you with a map of secure and beautiful places where you can camp and enjoy many nice views.
  • Click on your destination on the map and you will get all the details you need about it.
  • We also have an online store where you can find all the items you need for you journey. We have a diversity of clothes, shoes and camping materials from different brands and prices. But that’s not all of it.
  • If you want to go camping and you don’t have a lot of time to take care of the logistics, we got your back!
In fact, this platform enables you to contact the nearest police office to your desired destination and send them a request in order for you to camp officially and guarantee your safety. Yalla Kampi is the perfect tool for you to be ready for camping and not waste any unnecessary time to prepare for your adventure.
